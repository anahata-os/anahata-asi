The August 2, 2026 EU AI Act Deadline

On August 2, 2026, the strict obligations for high-risk AI systems under the European Union's EU AI Act officially become law. Clinical decision support software and automated trial-matching tools must prove continuous human oversight, detailed logging, and unalterable operation audits.

Anahata's local, Java-based runtime natively satisfies these mandates. Every automated action is gated behind a mandatory clinician approval window, and all operation metrics are written down to our unalterable Kryo flight recorder, providing immediate audit compliance.
